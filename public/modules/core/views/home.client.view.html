Open Congress

Play

Politics can be boring, and many people don't have difficulty summoning the will get involved. Others are very politically active, but don't know how to take their participation to the next level without sacrificing an unreasonable amount of their time. Open Congress is here to change that. With fun, interactive games for people at all levels of political activity, Open Congress will get you hooked on democracy, and make often tedious tasks like analysing voting records more fun than you ever thought possible.

Learn

When you use Open Congress, not only are you having fun, but you're learning a lot about the political landscape of the United States. Our information comes from the sunlight foundation's api, which means that the congressional data used to populate our games is accurate within a day - and in some cases even a minute - of the change

Get Involved

In addition to giving you information in game form with our constantly updated info from the sunlight foundation, We'll also give you the tools you need to get more involved, with integration of websites like govtrack.us and opensecrets. As well as features like being notified of congressional votes by email and texting your address to our phone number, and getting back your personalized congress info.
